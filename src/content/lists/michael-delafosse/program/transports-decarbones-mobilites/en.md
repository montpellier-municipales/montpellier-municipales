Michaël Delafosse continues Montpellier's ambition for free and sustainable public transport, while developing soft mobility.

### Key points:

1. **Fully Free Public Transport**: Maintaining and extending the TAM network's free access for all metropolis residents.
2. **Tram Line 5**: Launch of the 5th tram line project to serve northern neighbourhoods and new residential areas.
3. **Cycling Network**: Development of a secure and continuous cycling network across the city, with separated lanes and bike parking.
4. **Electric Buses**: Continuation of fleet greening with electric or hydrogen-powered vehicles.
5. **Intermodality**: Development of multimodal hubs to facilitate bike + tram + bus combinations and reduce private car use.
