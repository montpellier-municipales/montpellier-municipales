Michaël Delafosse places education and the development of children and young people at the heart of his vision for the city.

### Key points:

1. **Quality School Catering**: Maintaining municipal canteens with a growing share of local and organic produce, with pricing based on family income.
2. **After-School Care**: Expanding after-school provision (leisure centres, supervised study) to meet families' needs.
3. **School Renovation**: Multi-year programme for thermal renovation and upgrading of primary schools.
4. **Equal Opportunities**: Strengthening academic support programmes in priority neighbourhoods.
5. **Youth and Civic Engagement**: Developing youth centres, supporting student associations and civic initiatives of young Montpellier residents.
