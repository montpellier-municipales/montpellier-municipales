Michaël Delafosse bets on Montpellier's economic appeal to create jobs and strengthen local sectors, drawing on the territory's assets.

### Key points:

1. **Support for Local Businesses**: Strengthening local public procurement and social clauses in municipal contracts to favour local employment.
2. **French Tech and Digital**: Supporting the digital ecosystem and French Tech Montpellier, which accounts for thousands of skilled jobs.
3. **Campus and Research**: Developing university-business links and promoting start-ups from Montpellier's research labs.
4. **Local Commerce**: Protecting and supporting city-centre and neighbourhood businesses against e-commerce competition.
5. **Professional Integration**: Strengthening work integration social enterprises (SIAE) to support people far from employment.
