Michaël Delafosse's flagship project for his second term is the "City-Park": transforming Montpellier into a resilient city facing climate change, combining greening, energy sobriety, and urban adaptation.

### Key points:

1. **Green Corridors and Tree Canopy**: Planting an additional 100,000 trees and creating green corridors linking parks and neighbourhoods.
2. **Soil De-sealing**: Transforming car parks and mineral squares into planted spaces to combat urban heat islands.
3. **Solar Energy**: Development of photovoltaics on public buildings and support for local energy communities.
4. **Climate Adaptation**: Updated heat and flood plans, with fountains and misting systems in public space.
5. **Biodiversity Plan**: Protection of wetlands, riparian zones, and ecological corridors around the city.
