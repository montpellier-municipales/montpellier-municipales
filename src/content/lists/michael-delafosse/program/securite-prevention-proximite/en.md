Michaël Delafosse bets on a significant reinforcement of municipal policing and video surveillance, while maintaining a prevention component.

### Key points:

1. **100 Additional Officers**: Doubling of municipal police forces to reach 200 officers in the field.
2. **Doubling Cameras**: Expansion of the video surveillance network to cover all sensitive public spaces.
3. **Strengthened Night Presence**: Municipal police teams present 24/7 in priority areas.
4. **Delinquency Prevention**: Maintaining social mediation programmes and street educators in neighbourhoods.
5. **Police–Justice Coordination**: Strengthening cooperation with the national police and public prosecutor for handling incivilities and offences.
