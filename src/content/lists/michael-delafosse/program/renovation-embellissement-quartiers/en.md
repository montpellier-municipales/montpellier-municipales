Michaël Delafosse is launching a major renovation programme so that every neighbourhood in Montpellier benefits from concrete, visible improvements.

### Key points:

1. **Home Energy Renovation**: Support for housing co-ownerships and modest households to finance energy renovations, complementing national grants.
2. **Priority Neighbourhood Regeneration**: Continuation of the national urban renewal programme (ANRU) in neighbourhoods such as La Mosson, Pas-du-Loup, and Les Cévennes.
3. **Neighbourhood Centre Beautification**: Repair of pavements, signage, street furniture, and lighting in each local centre.
4. **Heritage and Historic Spaces**: Restoration of monuments, fountains, and public spaces in the historic centre.
5. **Universal Accessibility**: Bringing public spaces and municipal buildings into compliance for people with reduced mobility.
