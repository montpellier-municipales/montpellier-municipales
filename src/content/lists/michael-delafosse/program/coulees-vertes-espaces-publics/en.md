To give Montpellier a more pleasant and cooler living environment, Michaël Delafosse wants to make large green corridors the backbone of the city.

### Key points:

1. **Structural Green Corridors**: Development of wide tree-lined promenades connecting the city centre to major parks (Rimbaud, Méric, Montmaur, etc.).
2. **Greening School Yards**: Transformation of all school playgrounds into mixed spaces with grass, trees, and cool zones.
3. **Community Gardens**: Creation of new collective gardens in every neighbourhood, managed with and by residents.
4. **Calmed Public Spaces**: Redesigning squares and streets to promote conviviality, biodiversity, and animal welfare.
5. **Gentle Lighting and Nocturnal Biodiversity**: Adapting public lighting to preserve nocturnal wildlife while maintaining safety.
