Michaël Delafosse wants to make Montpellier a city where sport and culture unite people and radiate far beyond its borders.

### Key points:

1. **Support for Professional Clubs**: Supporting MHSC (football), MHB (handball), and other professional clubs in their development and local roots.
2. **Sport for All**: Developing local sports provision, maintaining time slots for associations, and renovating neighbourhood sports facilities.
3. **Living Culture**: Supporting the Opéra Comédie, the Montpellier National Orchestra, festivals (Printemps des Comédiens, Festival de Radio France), and neighbourhood cultural venues.
4. **International Influence**: Strengthening partnerships with festivals and cultural institutions to position Montpellier as the cultural capital of southern France.
5. **Cultural Access for All**: Solidarity pricing in municipal museums, theatres, and performance venues, and arts education programmes in schools.
