To keep Montpellier accessible to everyone, Michaël Delafosse proposes multiplying affordable housing solutions, particularly through the Real Solidarity Lease (BRS).

### Key points:

1. **Real Solidarity Lease (BRS)**: Massive rollout of the BRS to allow modest households to access homeownership without buying the land, with a purchase price reduced by 20 to 40%.
2. **Social Housing Construction**: Maintaining a sustained pace of social housing construction in neighbourhoods, particularly in high-pressure zones.
3. **Fight Against Vacant Housing**: Activating mechanisms to return vacant properties to the market, including tax incentives and owner support.
4. **Short-Term Rental Regulation**: Strengthening control of Airbnb-type rentals to preserve housing supply for permanent residents.
5. **Emergency Housing**: Developing emergency shelter places and support pathways to permanent housing for homeless people.
