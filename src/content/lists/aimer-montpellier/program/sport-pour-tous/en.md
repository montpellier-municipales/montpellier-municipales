Isabelle Perrein sees sport as a factor for social cohesion, effort, and respect. She wants to make it a driver for Montpellier's visibility.

- **Sports from School**: Regular practice and sports education for all children during extra-curricular times.
- **City Parks in Every District**: Outdoor sports areas for youth, PRM, and seniors, with health paths.
- **HandiPass Sport**: Free admission for the companion of a disabled person in all municipal sports facilities.
- **Montpellier Sport Pass**: Tournaments open to all and competitions between young talents and professionals during holidays.
- **Street Sports**: Street Ball, Futsal, and popular sports events directly in the streets.
- **Talent Identification from School**: Local work to find and support future champions.
- **Promotion of Local Athletes**: Campaigns and podcasts to highlight Montpellier's champions.
- **City of Sport**: A unique meeting place for clubs, sports schools, and local actors.
- **Adapted Facilities**: Sincere dialogue with clubs to support their infrastructure projects.
