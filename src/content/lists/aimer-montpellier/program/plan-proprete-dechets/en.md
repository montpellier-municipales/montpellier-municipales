Isabelle Perrein aims to make Montpellier a model of cleanliness and circular economy by ending current unsanitary conditions.

- **Adapted Cleaning Frequency**: Passage frequency of cleaning services reviewed according to each neighborhood's life.
- **Modern Sorting Columns**: Aesthetic and functional equipment with penalties for littering.
- **Clean Incinerator for Ultimate Waste**: A cost-effective and environmentally responsible facility.
- **Neighborhood Sorting Managers**: Staff dedicated to education and coordination of local sorting.
- **Massive Biowaste Collection**: Generalized composting to reduce trash volume.
- **Greywater Cleaning**: Using recycled water for street cleaning and irrigation.
- **Data Center Heat Recovery**: Using heat from local data centers for the city's heating network.
