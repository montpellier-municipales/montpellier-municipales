Isabelle Perrein proposes to boost social mobility in Montpellier by making the city the preferred partner for businesses to create accessible jobs.

- **Direct Business Dialogue**: Permanent communication with business leaders, artisans, and unions.
- **Local Commerce Support**: Improving security, accessibility, and cleanliness to foster local consumption.
- **Internship House**: A meeting place for businesses and youth looking for training or apprenticeships.
- **Public-Private Investment Fund**: A fund to strengthen the capital of local companies committing to hire in Montpellier.
- **Economic PLUI**: Protecting business zones to allow growing companies to stay in the city.
- **Excellence Hubs**: Promoting health, AI, renewable energy, and agro-food sectors to attract talent.
- **International High School**: Proposing a new school to facilitate international business recruitment.
- **Low-Qualification Jobs**: Attracting logistics and recycling companies to provide accessible employment.
- **Sustainable Digital Infrastructure**: Investing in data centers and modern digital tools.
