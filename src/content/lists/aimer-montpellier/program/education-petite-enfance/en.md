Isabelle Perrein proposes to make Montpellier a "City for Children and Youth," where everyone can grow and learn safely.

- **Local Daycare Centers**: Near business zones to simplify family life.
- **Support for Maternal Assistant Houses**: Valuing and assisting these structures.
- **Disability Inclusion from Daycare**: Human, qualified, and adapted care.
- **21st Century School**: Schools as complete educational centers with health professionals (speech therapists, psychologists, nurses).
- **Art and Sport Workshops**: Coordinated programs with sports clubs, operas, and museums.
- **Youth Support**: Innovative digital platform to discover jobs and training in video and locally.
- **Neighborhood Educational Activities**: Coordinated programs in public spaces during school holidays.
- **Prevention and Education**: Campaigns against drugs, violence, harassment, and health risks.
- **Better Working Conditions**: More training and dignity for early childhood staff.
