Isabelle Perrein proposes to place human vulnerability at the center of municipal action to ensure accessible health and lasting autonomy for all citizens.

- **Multidisciplinary Health Houses**: Facilities with health professionals in all sectors.
- **Easy Hospital Access**: 2 hours of free parking per day for patient families.
- **Universal Accessibility Technical Service**: A municipal unit to ensure 100% accessibility of public spaces.
- **Disability and Senior House**: A shared place to inform citizens about their rights and assist with procedures.
- **Dedicated Phone Line**: Administrative advice and prevention for vulnerable people.
- **Golden Age House**: An animated meeting place in public space to fight senior isolation.
- **Active Seniors Reserve**: A body of senior volunteers for intergenerational link creation.
- **Free City-Center Shuttles**: Specific transport for seniors and PRM.
- **Disability Referent in City Hall**: A single point of contact within municipal staff.
- **Home Care Support**: Reinforced assistance for seniors and elevator adaptation awareness.
- **"Senior" Parking Spaces**: Reserved parking near essential services.
