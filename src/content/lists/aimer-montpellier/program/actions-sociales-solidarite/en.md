Isabelle Perrein proposes concrete and transparent solutions to improve the daily lives of the most vulnerable and strengthen social cohesion.

- **Local Solidarity Grocery Stores**: Quality products at affordable prices in collaboration with local producers.
- **Reducing Water and Energy Bills**: Individual meters in collective housing and advantageous electricity contracts.
- **Cheaper Urban Heating**: 25% bill reduction through data center heat recovery.
- **Refocused CCAS**: Targeted social actions based on in-depth needs analysis.
- **Homeless Support**: Ensuring a roof, access to services, and support toward autonomy.
- **One-Stop Rights Shop**: Direct access to associations and public services for tailored support.
- **Neighborhood Association House**: An office for every association and shared space for activities.
- **Transparent Subsidy Allocation**: Ending "small arrangements" with clear performance-based conditions.
- **Crowdfunding Platform**: Local solutions to support citizen projects.
- **Reintegration Program**: Collaboration with animal protection associations for social reintegration.
- **Personal Consumption Monitoring**: Alerts to help households manage their water expenses.
