The professionalisation of politics has created a class of elected officials disconnected from the realities of working people. Révolution Permanente proposes returning power to the people.

### Key priorities:

- **Recallable elected officials**: any elected representative must be able to be recalled by their voters at any time, and must be paid a nurse's salary, with no accumulation of mandates.
- **Voting rights from age 16**: for all residents, regardless of nationality.
- **Macron's resignation and the end of the Fifth Republic**: ending institutions that concentrate power in the hands of a political elite and building a new Republic in the service of the people.
