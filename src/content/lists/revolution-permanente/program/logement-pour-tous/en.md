In Montpellier, 13,000 homes sit empty while thousands of people are homeless or living in unacceptable conditions. Révolution Permanente proposes radical measures to restore housing as a right.

### Key priorities:

- **Requisitioning empty homes**: commandeering the 13,000 vacant properties in Montpellier to house homeless people, victims of violence, and LGBTI+ people facing homophobia and transphobia.
- **Freezing and capping rents**: imposing strict rent controls on landlords to end speculation and make the city affordable for low-income households.
