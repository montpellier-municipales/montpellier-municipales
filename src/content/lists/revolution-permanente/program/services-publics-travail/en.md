Révolution Permanente opposes the gifts handed to employers and property developers at the expense of public services. Max Muller proposes redirecting public money towards the needs of workers.

### Key priorities:

- **Total end to employer subsidies**: no more grants to big companies or anti-ecological projects like the CGM or the CSR plastic incinerator; public money must go to public services, not shareholders.
- **Massive investment plan**: funded by large fortunes, for health, schools, transport, seniors and accessibility for people with disabilities.
- **Nurseries, laundries and community canteens**: opening local services to address the rising cost of living, over-indebtedness and inflation.
- **Permanent contracts for all municipal workers**: guaranteeing job security for all precarious public servants who are essential to the functioning of public services.
