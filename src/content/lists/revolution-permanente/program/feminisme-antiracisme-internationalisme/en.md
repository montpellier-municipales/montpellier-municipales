Révolution Permanente defends a city that breaks with police violence, guarantees women's rights and expresses solidarity with oppressed peoples around the world.

### Key priorities:

- **No to police violence**: disarming the municipal police, expelling the BAC, riot police and transport police from working-class neighbourhoods.
- **Women's right to control their own bodies**: "too covered or not enough — it is for women to decide" — defending the right to abortion and fighting all forms of bodily control.
- **International solidarity**: support for the Palestinian people, the Kurdish people and all oppressed peoples; ending the twinning between Montpellier and Israeli cities; ending repression against pro-Palestinian activists.
