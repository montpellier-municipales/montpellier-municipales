Rémi Gaillard proposes a safety approach based on human presence, useful technology, and concrete solidarity.

### Key points:

1.  **Emergency Terminals**: Installation of emergency call boxes connected to the Urban Supervision Center for immediate assistance.
2.  **Adaptive Lighting**: Enhancing public lighting to improve safety while optimizing energy use.
3.  **Night Mediation**: Deploying mediators to soothe public spaces and prevent conflicts.
4.  **Targeted Municipal Police**: A community-oriented, visible, and accessible police force.
5.  **Victim Protection**: Creating new emergency shelters for victims of violence and supporting their path to autonomy.
6.  **Dignity and Solidarity**: Specific measures to ensure the dignity of homeless people and combat exclusion.
