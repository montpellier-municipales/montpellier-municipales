Rémi Gaillard proposes a radical waste reduction policy to transform Montpellier into an exemplary circular economy city.

- **Support for Repair**: Promoting resource centers and repair workshops.
- **Generalized Composting**: Facilitating source separation of bio-waste for all residents.
- **Zero Single-Use Plastic**: Supporting shopkeepers and events in switching to sustainable alternatives.
- **Education**: Specific school programs to embed waste reduction habits from a young age.
