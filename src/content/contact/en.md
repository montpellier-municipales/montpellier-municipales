## Operation

The site is run by a **citizen collective**, operating voluntarily and independently.

We try to respond as quickly as possible, but depending on the period, some time may be necessary before getting a response.

---

## Data Protection

No personal data is collected via the site.

Messages sent by email are only used to process your request and are not stored or used for other purposes.

To learn more, you can consult our [privacy policy](/en/politique-confidentialite).
