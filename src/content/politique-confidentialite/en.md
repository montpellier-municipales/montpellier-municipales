# Privacy Policy

## Commitment to Privacy

The site **montpellier-municipales.fr** is committed to respecting the privacy of its users. Our approach is based on transparency and data minimization.

As a civic and volunteer project, we have no commercial interest in collecting your data.

---

## No Collection of Personal Data

We collect **no personal data** on this site.

*   **No registration forms:** You do not need to create an account to access the content.
*   **No newsletter:** We do not store email addresses.
*   **No comments:** The site does not allow direct posting of comments, thus avoiding the collection of identifiers or IP addresses.

---

## Cookies and Trackers

This site uses **no cookies** and **no trackers**.

*   **No advertising cookies.**
*   **No social media cookies.**
*   **No audience measurement tools** (like Google Analytics, Matomo, etc.) that place cookies on your device.

You can browse the entire site without being tracked.

---

## Hosting and Server Logs

The site is hosted by **Cloudflare Pages**.

Like any host, Cloudflare may technically collect standard connection logs (IP address, date of connection, pages visited) for security and service maintenance purposes (DDoS attack detection, etc.). These data are not used by the site's editorial team for profiling or marketing purposes.

For more information, you can consult Cloudflare's privacy policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

---

## Links to Third-Party Sites

The site may contain links to other websites (official sources, press articles, candidate list sites).

If you click on these links, you leave **montpellier-municipales.fr**. We have no control over these external sites, which may have their own privacy policies and use cookies. We invite you to consult their respective legal notices.
